Defence Gold Souk

Defence Gold Souk is a premier shopping destination located in the heart of the city. It offers a wide range of gold and diamond jewelry, as well as luxury goods and services.

Project Overview & Structure


Building Height:
6 Storeys (Ground + 5 Floors).
Total Capacity: Designed to house approximately
60 premium shops. Target Niche:Gold, Silver, Diamonds, Bullion, and Luxury Watches.

The building is designed to provide a luxurious shopping experience, with spacious layouts and high-end finishes throughout.
